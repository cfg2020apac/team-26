Serve-a-Thon

Serve-a-thon was born in 2017 after nearly a decade of the HandsOn team serving the Hong Kong community and witnessing first-hand that the majority of citizens would like to volunteer but often need help to get started. With a record 1.37 million people living below the poverty line—existing on as little as HK$4,000 per month—the role Hong Kong non-profits play is more important than ever. They work tirelessly to serve the city's most vulnerable but cannot do it alone.

Hike For Hong Kong--An annual walk supporting local community

HandsOn Hong Kong was delighted to once again partner with the Listing Committee of the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong on our annual Hike for Hong Kong event held on November 2, 2019. Participants enjoyed a beautiful morning hiking 7.5km on Section 4 of the Hong Kong trail in support of the local community. Funds raised from this year's hike will enable a full year of financial literacy programs to support the disadvantaged in Hong Kong.

Race The Peak

HandsOn is delighted to be chosen once again as the beneficiary charity for the family-friendly Race the Peak scheduled for April 19, 2020. Everyone taking part will raise funds to help us continue to manage up to 200 community volunteer programs each month as a free public service—at no cost to our NGO partners or volunteers. Runners will start and finish at The Peak Galleria and will be able to join a 7K Challenge, 1K Kids Dash and a 3.5K Family Run/Walk. Volunteers are also needed to help on the day as Race Marshals, to hand out water, distribute medals, manage the information area and bag check among other essential tasks.
